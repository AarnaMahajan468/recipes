Palak Paneer

Description

Soft cubes of fresh cottage cheese sank in a smooth, deeply aromatic, and vibrant green spinach gravy

The following recipe is not intended to be followed and is for project purposes

Ingredients

  • Spinach (Palak)
  • Paneer
  • Onion
  • Ginger and Garlic Paste
  • Garam Masala

Steps

  1. Blanch the Spinach
  2. Saute the Aromatics
  3. Bloom the Spices
  4. Simmer Sauce
  5. Add Paneer
