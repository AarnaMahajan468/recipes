Rajma

Description

Plump red kidney beans slow-simmered in a thick, comforting, and fiercely seasoned onion-tomato gravy.

The following recipe is not intended to be followed and is for project purposes

Ingredients

  • Kidney beans(Rajma)
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Rajma masala powder

Steps

  1. Boil the Beans
  2. Cook Aromatics
  3. Cook the Tomatoes
  4. Add Spices and Mash
  5. Combine and Simmer
